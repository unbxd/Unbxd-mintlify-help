---
title: 'Collections'
description: 'Ability to upload products and enhance them with custom attributes for landing pages'
---

Netcore Unbxd Collections enable eCommerce businesses to create custom landing pages effortlessly, showcasing curated product listings without modifying the catalog. This feature is ideal for promotions and events, allowing businesses to launch targeted campaigns quickly. With an intuitive interface, it streamlines landing page creation and management for timely and effective merchandising.
